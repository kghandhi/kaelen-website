About Me

Kaelen is making a musical difference in the world. As a New York based musician and teacher he is interested in bringing us together in a way only music can. He specializes in jazz playing primarily the tenor saxophone, but he is skilled in all saxophones and the flute—he is a constant learner and is working on clarinet competence.
